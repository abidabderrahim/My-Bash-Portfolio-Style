# Bash Style Developer Portfolio

This portfolio is designed in **Bash style**, showcasing skills, experience, and projects as commands, variables, and functions. It gives a **terminal-like feel**, appealing to CLI-savvy developers.

## Key Features

- **Command-Line Presentation:**  
  - Skills, experience, and projects are displayed as variables and functions.
  - Functions describe work experience, education, and certifications.
  - Projects are represented as clickable links similar to commands.

- **Interactive Elements:**  
  - Email, phone, LinkedIn, GitHub, and location are clickable.
  - Projects link directly to live repositories.

- **Organized Layout:**  
  - Left column: Profile & Skills.  
  - Right column: Summary, Info, Experience, Education, Projects, Certifications, Hobbies.
  - Terminal-style formatting makes it easy to scan at a glance.

- **Developer-Friendly Design:**  
  - Perfect for developers familiar with CLI environments and Bash scripting.
  - Reflects **practical skills, technical knowledge, and attention to detail**.

## Benefits for Developers

- Provides a **terminal-style overview** of your skills and experience.
- Clickable links allow easy access to **projects and contact info**.
- Unique Bash-style design sets your portfolio apart from traditional resumes.
- Ideal for technical recruiters and developers looking for a **developer-centric portfolio**.

This portfolio is perfect for developers who love **CLI and shell scripting aesthetics** while showcasing their skills professionally.
